Lentil and Veg Soup

Home

Description

Tackle the challange of eating convenient, healthy and budget friendly food. This creamy vegetable and lentil soup is packed with nutrients while tasting like comfort in a bowl.

It's perfect for busy people as it only takes 20 minutes to cook and as a bonus for fusy eaters, blending gives a palatable uniform texture, you wouldn't even believe that vegitables were hiding in there!

Ingredients

  • Frozen mixed root vegetables
  • Dried red split lentils
  • Vegetable stock
  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Rinse 1/2 cup of dried lentils with cold water until the water runs clear
  2. Bring lentils to boil and let simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Fill a pot with frozen vegatables and simmer
  4. Add Vegetable stck to vegetables
  5. Blend vegetables and lentils until smooth
  6. Serve with buttered bread
